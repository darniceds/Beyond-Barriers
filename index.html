Our Story

In a world where language divides, where words are lost in translation, there exists a shared experience among those who have felt the weight of silence, the frustration of misunderstanding. Beyond Barriers was born from this shared struggle, from the desire to bridge the gap and find connection amidst the chaos of linguistic diversity.

Beyond Barriers was established in 2024. We are an international team of non native speaking expats.We have all had barriers related to language. We wanted to create a tool which would give freedom and independence that we wish we had to the others coming behind us.

Be Independent. Be Free. Be Beyond Barriers.
